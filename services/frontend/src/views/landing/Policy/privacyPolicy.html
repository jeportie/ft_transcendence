Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 6, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. We collect the minimum data needed to make Transcendence run smoothly. We don’t sell your data, we don’t stalk you, and we certainly don’t train your toaster with it.

1) What We Collect

  • • Account data: username, email, profile basics.
  • • Auth data: salted + hashed passwords, 2FA status, OAuth IDs (scoped).
  • • Game/app telemetry: non-sensitive usage events to improve UX.
  • • Technical logs: IP, user agent, timestamps (for security & debugging).

2) What We Don’t Collect

  • • We don’t store plain-text passwords. Ever.
  • • We don’t read your private chats for fun. Moderation happens only if required.
  • • We don’t sell or rent your data. No secret marketplace, no shadowy auctions.

3) Why We Collect It

  • • To log you in and keep your session secure (JWTs, cookies, 2FA).
  • • To run core features (leaderboards, matchmaking, cosmetics, etc.).
  • • To fix bugs, fight abuse, and improve performance.

4) Cookies & Storage

We use cookies/localStorage for sessions and preferences. You can clear them, but you might get logged out or lose some settings (like your favorite theme: “Dark but Fancy”).

5) Data Sharing

We may use trusted processors (hosting, email, analytics) bound by data-protection agreements. We share data only when necessary to provide the service or comply with the law. No surprise parties.

6) Security

We use industry best practices: hashing (e.g., Argon2), transport encryption (HTTPS), least-privilege access, and monitoring. No system is 100% hack-proof, but we try very hard. If you spot a vulnerability, responsible disclosure gets you infinite gratitude (and probably swag).

7) Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes above, or as required by law. When you delete your account, we remove or anonymize personal data, keeping just what’s necessary for security, accounting, or legal compliance.

8) Your Rights

  • • Access your data.
  • • Correct your data.
  • • Delete your data (subject to legal/operational constraints).
  • • Object or restrict processing in certain cases.

To exercise these rights, contact us via the app. We’ll verify it’s really you (sorry, cat).

9) International Transfers

If data crosses borders, we’ll use appropriate safeguards (standard contractual clauses, etc.).

10) Changes

We may update this policy as Transcendence evolves. We’ll post updates here and adjust the “Last updated” date. Major changes get a heads-up.

11) Contact

Privacy questions? Ping us via the app’s support. We read every message (with coffee).
